About BONZ Coin

When someone buys something using BONZ, the artist or band gets paid instantly — no credit card processing fees, no 3-day bank holds, and no middleman taking a cut. It’s direct. It’s fast. And it’s built specifically for music.

The more people use BONZ, the more valuable the coin becomes. So fans who support the project early — by simply holding BONZ — could benefit as the ecosystem grows.

For bands, joining early means receiving a starter supply of BONZ tokens — basically free — which they can either hold as the project grows, or use creatively to engage their fans. For example, a band could say: “Anyone holding 100 BONZ gets access to an unreleased track,” or “Fans who pay with BONZ get exclusive perks.”

It’s a way to build a deeper connection with supporters — fans feel like insiders, and artists stay fully in control.

💡 Real-world example: A band sells a digital album using BONZ. A smart contract instantly handles the payment and unlocks access to the download — no waiting, no approval needed. After the show, it could automatically send a recording to buyers, or even allow token holders to vote on future setlists. It’s automation, accountability, and creativity — all rolled into one.

With BONZ, fans feel like insiders, artists stay in control, and the entire experience is faster, fairer, and more connected.
